# Display

Display utilities classes are used to control the display of an element.
They can be used to show or hide elements, change their visibility, and control their layout.

Avaliable display utilities:

- `block`
- `inline`
- `inline-block`
- `none`
- `flex`
- `inline-flex`
- `grid`
- `inline-grid`
- `table`
- `table-row`
- `table-cell`
