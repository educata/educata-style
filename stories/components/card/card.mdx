# Card

There is two card component.

- [Promo Card](#promo-card)
- [Simple Card](#simple-card)

## Promo card

Promo card contains a title, description, and a cta. It has gradient color for background and border.

<Canvas of={CardStories.PromoCardWithoutImage} className='bg-main' />

Promo card base color could be change via `promo-card-{{COLOR}}`.

<Canvas of={CardStories.PromoCardWithoutImage2} className='bg-main' />

Avaliable colors are:

- white
- black
- blue
- red
- green
- purple
- yellow

### With image

Promo card could have an image. The image is on the right bottom side of the card.
